busybox-w32 is NOT bundled with AIASys. It is offered as an optional,
user-initiated download from the in-app "Environment Enhancements" panel for
Windows users who do not have Git Bash or WSL available. When downloaded, it
runs as a separate external process invoked by the AIASys shell executor.

Because it is a GPL-2.0 component, the full license text is included here, and
users who install it through AIASys are informed of its license and source-code
availability before the download begins.
